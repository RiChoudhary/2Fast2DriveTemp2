About the Project

The purpose of our project is to create a fully autonomous racing vehicle capable of going around a track as quickly as possible. The goal is to have the vehicle drive around a race track, avoid collisions with objects, and potentially compete in the annual F1Tenth International Competition. The F1tenth vehicle must be able to complete multiple laps around any given track. The vehicle should use the sensors to identify the layout of the environment and determine when to move forward or to start turning.
